Financial Services

Platform Approach led to Increase Digital Lending & Product Innovation

The bank wanted to create a unified and high-performing platform that would provide an integrated view of the entire retail portfolio while bringing all stakeholders on the same page to infuse the nimbleness of a digital-native fintech company into their legacy banking systems.

We partnered with Axis to develop a rich LOS platform with e2e integration with existing systems with New/Reimagined customer journeys. As a result of which they experienced 44% growth in loan disbursement and 75% faster time to market.

Retail

In 2018, we helped them kickoff their transformation journey with a consulting engagement delivering a blueprint and roadmap to create a customer retail platform. Since then, we have partnered with them to on multiple programmes including custom eCommerce platform, Marketplace, Custom POS, and Logistics. And recently we converted an opportunity with their Finance business, to work on Payments.

We have started Business Agility program in 2021 to help them evolve their operating model better suited for a digital business (MDB).

This has helped accelerate their adoption of emerging touch points and operating models(like marketplace) of future and potentially open up additional non-linear streams of revenue

Financial Services

The bank aims to leverage technology to meet its stated vision, exponentially expand its capability & reach, and compete with industry incumbents. Thoughtworks Implemented digital-first strategy and developed a cloud-based hyper-personalization platform to define customer DNA for a differentiated experience. Released 650+ features covering essential banking services leading to one-click customer onboarding (reduced from 2 days to real-time) and made IDFC FIRST the first Indian bank to do weekly releases.
