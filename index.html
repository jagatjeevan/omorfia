Platform Approach led to Increase Digital Lending & Product Innovation

The bank wanted to create a unified and high-performing platform that would provide an integrated view of the entire retail portfolio while bringing all stakeholders on the same page to infuse the nimbleness of a digital-native fintech company into their legacy banking systems.
